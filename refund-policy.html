Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

NPB Media is committed to customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be requested and processed for our software development and related services.

2. Project-Based Services

For fixed-scope projects:

  • Before project start: A full refund may be issued if you cancel before any work has begun, minus any non-refundable administrative fees disclosed at the time of agreement.
  • After project start: Refunds are prorated based on work completed. No refund is available once the project has been delivered and accepted.
  • Milestone-based projects: Payments for completed milestones are non-refundable. Unused milestone payments may be refunded at our discretion.

3. Ongoing and Subscription Services

For maintenance, support, or subscription-based services:

  • Refunds may be issued for the unused portion of the current billing period if you cancel within the first 7 days of the subscription.
  • No refunds are provided for partial months or for services already rendered.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us at er.raushan.ec@gmail.com with "Refund Request" in the subject line.
  2. Include your project or order reference, reason for the request, and any supporting documentation.
  3. We will review your request and respond within 5–10 business days.
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10–15 business days.

5. Exceptions

Refunds are generally not available for:

  • Services already completed and accepted
  • Custom-developed software that has been delivered
  • Third-party costs (licenses, hosting, etc.) already incurred

6. Contact Us

For refund inquiries, contact us at er.raushan.ec@gmail.com or +91 8877300114.
